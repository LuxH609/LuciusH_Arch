THE NOCTURNE

Ladies and Gentlemen, I am pleased to introduce to you the groundbreaking project in New York - "The Nocturne: Continental Hotel."

This project aims to combine vampire culture with modern technology, creating an extraordinary bio-tech spectacle. As a haven and gathering place for vampires, this building merges cutting-edge technology with biotechnology. From the exterior to the interior, it exudes a unique Gothic taste. Our guest rooms are the epitome of luxury. Each room is equipped with state-of-the-art bio-tech facilities, including daylight protection systems and smart home devices. Whether it's daytime secret slumber or nighttime revelry, our guests can enjoy unparalleled comfort and safety.

In this hotel, you are not just a guest but also a distinguished member of the vampire community. The Nocturne is luxurious and unique, your journey into the trendy vampire nightlife of New York starts here.


PROJECT INFO

Location: 180 Maiden Ln, NY 10038, USA

Project Type: Parametric design based on existing building condition

Building Type: Commercial & Office (before), Hotel & Commercial (after)

Design Period: January 2024 - now

Main Tools: Grasshopper (Kangaroo, Lunchbox et. al), Rhino, Sub-D, Vray, Arduino

Instructor: Anthony Samaha
